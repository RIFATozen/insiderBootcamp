Ezel

Betrayed by his trusted friends and the woman he loved, Ömer Uçar returns as Ezel to exact his vengeance.

Explore Characters

Main Characters

Ezel Bayraktar

Previously known as Ömer Uçar, he returns with a new identity to seek revenge against those who betrayed him.

Eyşan Tezcan

Ömer's former love interest who betrayed him. Her actions set the main plot in motion.

Cengiz Atay

A cunning businessman who was one of the masterminds behind Ömer's downfall.

Kerpeten Ali

Ali is a childhood friend of Ömer Uçar (later Ezel Bayraktar) and they worked together in an auto repair shop.

Ramiz Dayı

A wise mentor figure who helps Ömer become Ezel and guides him through his journey of revenge.

Bahar

A kind-hearted woman who falls in love with Ezel, unaware of his true identity and mission.

Some Important Episodes

Episode 1

Every Betrayal Begins with Love

Ezel and his team arrive to Cyprus to avenge Ömer, who has been betrayed by his best friends in 1997, twelve years ago.

Episode 9

The Worst Sase Scenario

Final Showdown

The series reaches its climactic conclusion as all secrets are revealed and everyone must face the consequences of their actions.
